Our Story

At Grace & Glory Hair Studio, hair is the ultimate expression of identity. Founded by Tshegofatso Grace Ngomazana, on the principles of precision installation and restorative care, we engineer and cater styles that protect your natural health while delivering a flawless personalized look. With the motivation and aspiration to ensure everyone deserves a beautiful experience, we strive to deliver on every occasion, now and forever.

From our primary location at Belgium Campus ITversity to our convenient and accommodating services in Ga-Rankuwa, we prioritize your comfort and confidence above all else. Our journey has been one of intentional growth—evolving from a local mission into a versatile, client-centered experience that brings our expertise directly to you, wherever you feel most at ease.
